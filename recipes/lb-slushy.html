Lemon Berry Slushy

Description

A slushy made with lemons and berries

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries, thawed, with juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tray ice cubes

Steps

  1. Put ingredients into a blender until slushy
  2. Serve in a tall glass with a straw
