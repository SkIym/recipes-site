Watermelon Cooler Slushy

Description

A slushy made with watermelons

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 10 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Put watermelon and ice into a blender
  2. Pour lime, add sugar and salt
  3. Blend until smooth
